# How to make teams (for captains)

**Newtown Sporting Club:** https://kick-draft.vercel.app/newtown-sporting-club  
**Captain's guide (with pictures):** https://kick-draft.vercel.app/guide

---

## In 7 steps

### 1. Open your squad
Go to **Newtown Sporting Club**:  
https://kick-draft.vercel.app/newtown-sporting-club

### 2. Name the match
Example: **July 7 - Suresh**  
This name is used when you share the lineup.

### 3. Tick who is coming
Under **Today's availability**, tap the checkbox for each player who is coming or will show up.  
**Manual only** — nobody is picked at random.

| Players selected | Match size |
|------------------|------------|
| 10 | 5v5 |
| 11 | 6v5 |
| 12 | 6v6 |
| 14 | 7v7 |

### 4. Balance teams
Tap **Balance teams**. The app splits your selection using:

- **OVR** — average of all seven stats including **stamina**
- **Stamina leaders** — top 4 stamina players → **2 per side**
- **Bookend draft** (6v6, 7v7) — strong and weak players paired across teams
- **Handicap** (6v5, 7v6) — smaller side gets a rating boost
- **Goalkeepers** — one per side when possible

### 5. Check the lineup
Team A vs Team B on the pitch with formation (e.g. 5v5 = 1-2-2).  
Tap a player to see their card — **STA** is stamina.

### 6. Not happy? Shuffle or edit

**Shuffle again** — same players, completely new auto-balanced teams, new link.

**Edit teams** — two modes:

| Mode | What you do |
|------|-------------|
| **Lock & shuffle** | Place a few players on Team A or B, tap **Fill rest of teams** — app balances the remainder |
| **Manual** | Assign every player yourself (arrows or drag). Nothing auto-filled. |

### 7. Share on WhatsApp
Tap **Share match**. You get:
- **Image** — pitch with both teams
- **Text** — match name, size, date, and one link

Example message:
```
July 7 - Suresh
5v5 · 5 Jul 2026, 13:01
https://kick-draft.vercel.app/newtown-sporting-club/match/...
```

---

## Tips
- Select **9–22 players** for a match
- Stamina (STA) is in OVR and in the top-4 stamina split
- Shuffle again = new teams; Edit teams = you control some or all placements
- Past matches are in **Match history** (30 days)
