# How to make teams (for captains)

**App:** https://kick-draft.vercel.app  
**Full guide with pictures:** https://kick-draft.vercel.app/guide

---

## In 6 steps

### 1. Open your squad
Go to the app and open your club page.

### 2. Name the match
Example: **July 7 - Suresh**  
This name is used when you share the lineup.

### 3. Tick who showed up
Under **Today's availability**, tap the checkbox next to each player.  
**Manual only** — nobody is picked at random.

| Players selected | Match size |
|------------------|------------|
| 10 | 5v5 |
| 11 | 6v5 |
| 12 | 6v6 |

### 4. Balance teams
Tap **Balance teams**. The app splits your selection into two fair sides.

### 5. Check the lineup
Team A vs Team B on the pitch with formation (e.g. 5v5 = 1-2-2).

### 6. Share on WhatsApp
Tap **Share match**. You get:
- **Image** — pitch with both teams
- **Text** — match name, size, date, and one link

Example message:
```
July 7 - Suresh
5v5 · 5 Jul 2026, 13:01
https://kick-draft.vercel.app/newtown-sporting-club/match/...
```

---

## Tips
- Select **9–22 players** for a match
- Tap a player on the pitch to see their stats
- Past matches are in **Match history** (30 days)
